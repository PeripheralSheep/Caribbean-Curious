Places to Stay in Barbados

When travelling to a different country, it's only obvious that you want to find the best places to stay. We will recommend some hotels, villas, guesthouses and resorts to stay while you visit Barbados.

SANDALS Barbados

This beautiful hotel is located in St. Lawrence Gap, Christ Church.

Sandals offers a restaurant, free private parking, an outdoor swimming pool as well as a fitness center. All rooms have air conditioning and a flat-screen TV

You can play billiard, table tennis and darts as well! The surrounding area is also popular for snorkeling and canoeing.

Visit their website to book and learn more

The Crane Resort

The Crane is the oldest continuously operation hotel in the Caribbean. Located in St. Philip, it offers perfect luxury amenities for over a century.

Services it offers are cascading cliff-top pools, award-winning restaurants and the world renowned Crane beach which is known as one of the most beautiful beaches in the world.

Visit their website to learn all about The Crane.

Crystal Springs Villa

This is a beautiful private estate on 1.7-acres of land. It is bright, spacious, comfortable yet stylish and is located on the beachfront. You can relax next to a pool or relax on one of the terraces.

It has access to a secluded sandy cove, an 18-ft speedboat, a Sunfish sailboat and some paddle boards. Nearby activites to the villa include tennis, golf and polo.

Like the other locations, visit their website for more information.
